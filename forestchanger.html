Post-Classification Natural Forest Change Analysis

R Markdown, Remote Sensing Coursework & Research

The embedded R Markdown file shows a post-classification change analysis between two land cover maps that I created for Mapping Drivers of Ecosystem Change in Colombia, a remote sensing research project I provide support to at Temple University.

The end result is a raster, frequency dataframe, and bar chart representing the area of land that was classified as natural dense forest in 2007 but was classified as another land cover in 2020.
