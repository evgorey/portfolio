Static Maps for the Temple University Facilities Information Management & Resources Office

When working for Temple University's Facilities Information Management & Resources (FIRM) office in July 2019, I designed two maps to represent the locations of the Temple University Kornberg Dental School's alumni within the Delaware Valley Region. Two versions were made to allow for variety, and both maps were designed using ArcGIS Pro.
